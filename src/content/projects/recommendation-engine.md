## Problem
On-site product discovery relied on static merchandising, leaving personalization opportunities on the table across a large catalog and diverse customer base.

## Method
The engine applies ML-based recommendation logic — combining behavioral signals, product attributes, and purchase history — to power personalized on-site discovery and engagement surfaces.

## System / Workflow
Recommendation models score and rank products per user context, served into the e-commerce experience. An in-house A/B testing framework validates every model and product change before full rollout, keeping decisions evidence-based.

## Impact
The experimentation infrastructure built for this system supported a checkout funnel fix that lifted conversion rate from 0.78% to 1.2%, and the recommendation logic now drives personalized discovery in production.

## Future Research Direction
Future work includes sequence-aware recommendation models, real-time session personalization, and bandit-based approaches that reduce the cost of experimentation.

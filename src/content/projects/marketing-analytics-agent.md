## Problem
Marketing teams need faster access to performance insights, segment behavior, experiment results, and recommended next actions.

## Method
The agent uses structured analytics workflows, natural language interfaces, and task-specific reasoning to answer campaign and customer questions.

## System / Workflow
Users ask questions, the agent identifies relevant data and analysis paths, executes approved workflows, summarizes evidence, and proposes follow-up actions.

## Impact
The system can reduce analysis cycle time and help teams move from reporting to decision-making.

## Future Research Direction
Future research can focus on agent evaluation, guardrails for business analytics, memory for longitudinal campaign context, and multi-agent planning.

## Problem
Organizations often build disconnected AI tools. A coherent platform is needed to orchestrate agents, models, context, evaluation, and business workflows.

## Method
OrbitAI explores modular AI services, reusable workflow primitives, and governance patterns for enterprise AI applications.

## System / Workflow
The system connects data sources, model services, agent tools, evaluation checkpoints, and human approval paths through a common orchestration layer.

## Impact
The platform direction supports reusable AI capabilities and faster movement from research concepts to production-grade workflows.

## Future Research Direction
Future work can study agent orchestration, evaluation-driven deployment, workflow memory, and decision provenance.
